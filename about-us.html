About the company

Empowering Opportunities

Upcountry Manpower Agency Ltd., established in 1988 under the Companies Act, is licensed by the Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment. Based in the historic hill capital of Sri Lanka, renowned for its cultural heritage and tourist attractions like the Maligawa, our agency serves clients worldwide.

General conditions

U.M.A. Enterprises (Private) Limited

Under the provisions of the Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment Act. No: 21 of 1985, (Ministry of Labour) all Sri Lankan migrant workers who secures overseas employment should register with the Sri Lanka Bureau of Foreign Employment prior to his /her departure from the country. Upon this registration, the migrant worker is qualified for some benefits, and for the purpose of this registration, following documents should be issued by the foreign employer to the recruiting agent in Sri Lanka.

A mutual understanding reached between parties, often formalized in writing.

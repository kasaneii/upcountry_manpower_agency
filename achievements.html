the company’s achievements

17 Years of Excellence

During the last 17 years, the company supplied manpower of various categories to countries in Middle East and Far East.In an independent survey conducted on behalf of the Ministry of Labour and Foreign Employment, Upcountry Manpower Agency (Pvt) Ltd was adjudged the Most Outstanding Foreign Employment Agency in Sri Lanka & was presented the Gold Award.

awards

Gold Award

Won the Gold Award for the Most Outstanding Foreign Employment Agency in Sri Lanka 2001.

02

Silver Award

Won the Silver Award for the Highest number of Skilled Employees in Sri Lanka 2001.

03

Silver Award

Won the Silver Award for the Widest Number of Markets in Sri Lanka 2001.
